CADERAS POWER BOWL

Description

A delicious home cooked meal, full of nutrients and everything you need. A power bowl to satisfy all of your needs. Includes protein, rice, veggies, sauce and an egg roll

Ingredients

    Chicken
    Rice
    Green Onions
    Carrots
    Potatoes
    Thin Pasta
    Secret Sauce

Steps

    Cook rice first in rice cooker
    Start cooking chicken or source of protein
    Cut up veggies
    Warm up pasta
    Finish cooking meat
    Add all items into bowl
    Add sauce and mix until it's all covered
