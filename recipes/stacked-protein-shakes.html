STACKED PROTEIN SHAKE

Description

Stacked protein shake full of goods. Perfect for busy mornings or late nights. Be ready whenever with this protein shake.

Ingredients

    Protein Powder
    Water
    Shake Bottle
    Bananas
    Chia Seeds
    Peanut Butter
    Spinach

Steps

    Add water to bottle
    Put 1 cup of protein powder into the bottle
    Cut up bananas
    Add chia seeds
    Add a spoonful of peanut butter
    Add spinach
    Run blender until it's well blended
