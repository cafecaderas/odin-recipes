FRIED RICE

Description

A healthy and packed dish. A fan favorite that cant be beat. Rice , the original and classic.

Ingredients

    Rice Cooker
    1 Cup Rice
    2 Cups Water
    Frozen Veggies
    Olive Oil
    Spices

Steps

    Clean rice
    Add veggies
    Add spices + olive oil
    Add 1 cup of rice
    Add 2 cups of water
    Run cooker for 45 minutes
    Mix rice 5 minutes before timer finishes
    Enjoy rice
